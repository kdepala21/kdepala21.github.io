Academics

 I focus on three main areas of research:

Sanskrit and Vernaculars

I read, translate, interpret, and make editions of texts written in Sanskrit, Pali, Gujarati, and Hindi, among other languages. 

Hinduism & Rituals

My PhD work focusses on the production of Hindu ritual texts and practices, especially those performed by large North Indian devotional movements.

Digital Humanities

I engage with tools and frameworks such as TEI encoding, image processing, OCR, digital editions.
